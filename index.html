My Story

The Journey of Sunil Kumar

A story of hard work, struggles, family, and dreams.

My name is Sunil Kumar. I was born on 2 August 1995 in Kauli, Patiala. I come from a simple family and have always believed that success comes through hard work and dedication.

I completed my schooling from Kauli School under PSEB and later pursued +2 and ITI (Fitter) from Samana. Like many people, my journey was not easy. I faced many challenges and difficult times, but every struggle taught me valuable lessons.

My professional career started at BinaryData Pvt. Ltd.. Over time, I developed a strong interest in technology and web development. Through continuous learning and practice, I became a Web Designer & Developer.

Family is the most important part of my life. My wife and my baby girl are my biggest motivation. Their love and support inspire me to work harder every day and build a better future.

I enjoy listening to music and working on creative web projects. My dream is to achieve greater success in my career, provide the best life for my family, and continue growing as a person and professional.

My journey is still ongoing. Every challenge makes me stronger, every achievement motivates me further, and every new day brings new opportunities to learn, grow, and move closer to my goals.
